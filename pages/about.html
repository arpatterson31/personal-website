Hi! I'm Audrey

I'm a Coding Instructor and Software Developer. In March of 2021, I decided to take a leap and made a career change. I completed an intense, fast-paced full-stack web development course that sharpened my coding skills but also ignited my love for teaching others. As an instructor, I develop and deliver engaging coding lessons, exercises, and projects. It's fulfilling to see my students gain practical skills and dive headfirst into the world of programming. Teaching is all about teamwork and collaboration. Working with my fellow instructors to constantly improve our curriculum and teaching methodologies is not only fun but also an opportunity for growth and creativity. Learning is a lifelong journey, and I'm all about embracing it! Being part of the tech community keeps my curiosity alive and fuels my drive to excel in what I do. I'm excited about new opportunities where I can continue to learn and grow as a developer, mentor, and teacher, leveraging my skills in JavaScript and React. Let's explore how my instructional and coding skills can bring value to your development team.
